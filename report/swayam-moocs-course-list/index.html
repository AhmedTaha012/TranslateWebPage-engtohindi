[2023] SWAYAM + NPTEL Courses Full List

Up-to-date list of the 900 free online courses currently offered on Swayam, India’s national course platform.

  • Facebook

Swayam is India’s national online course platform, designed to support the country’s National Education Policy, which entails improving access to quality education in India.

Since its beta launch in August 2016, 30 million learners have registered on Swayam. At the rate it’s growing, it could one day become a contender for the world’s largest online course platform.

Swayam offers courses in a wide variety of disciplines, from prestigious institutions such as the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and Central Universities.

In this article, I’ve compiled the 900 courses currently open for registration on Swayam.

Platform & Offering

Class Central has been keeping track of Swayam’s evolution since the platform was first announced in August 2014.

In July 2019, SWAYAM’s homepage and course catalog were revamped, among other to include all courses offered by NPTEL, a joint initiative of some of India’s most prominent engineering institutions.

All Swayam courses can be taken for free. Students can opt to pay a small fee (about $15) to sit a proctored exam in an exam center in India to earn a certificate of completion.

These certificates can be used by students in India to earn academic credit. Indeed, universities can earmark Swayam courses as credit-eligible. If you’d like to know how Swayam fits with India’s higher education, head to our previous article: In India, MOOCs Are Now Part of the Education System.

In 2020, Indian universities also started offering online degrees. Class Central has compiled a list: 500+ UGC Approved Online Degrees from India’s Top Universities.

Finally, students in India may also complete part of their degree online via Swayam. Since 2021, students may earn up to 40% of the academic credit required for graduation by taking relevant courses on Swayam.

My Experience

I personally completed several courses on Swayam, including an NPTEL course during the last semester of my master’s degree in computer science (specialization in AI) at the Central University of Rajasthan, India.

I paid a small extra fee (less than $2) to receive by mail the certificate that you can see below. Paper certificates have been discontinued, but students can still obtain virtual certificates that afford the same privileges.

I joined the Class Central engineering team in April 2019, and part of my job is to make sure that Class Central’s catalog of over 100K courses is up-to-date. This means that I develop and maintain scrapers that collect course information from different providers.

And I am happy to say that we list all Swayam courses. There are close to 900 courses live on Swayam right now. You can find the full list below. If you want to be kept in the loop about the platforms new courses, visit the Swayam page and click the Follow button.
