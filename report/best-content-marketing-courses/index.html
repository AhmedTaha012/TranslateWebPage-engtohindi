What is Content Marketing?

Content marketing is the creation and distribution of content in order to attract readers and viewers for your company or product. It can be used in different ways: written blog posts, videos for YouTube or social media, podcasts, or really any other format that helps your audience take action.

The main advantage of content marketing over paid advertising is that you can build trust by giving valuable information and guidance to your customers from the very start. In that sense, content marketing creates a win-win situation for everyone involved. As long as you create high quality content and use the right strategies to rank on search engines, you are set to win.

That’s why it’s important for content marketers, writers, and marketing professionals to align what is the real purpose of any campaign. It’s tempting to focus on increasing the spike when we first share our content to our current audience in search of the most viral content, but in reality, steadily increasing because you’ve ranked well on Google and getting organic traffic over time, might be just as rewarding.
