In a previous Class Central article, we compiled more than 600 free certificate courses and badges offered by Google. In this article, we focus on Google Cloud certifications and training courses.

Google Certifications

The Google Cloud Platform (GCP) is a suite of cloud computing services offered by Google. It includes over 100 products and services such as compute, storage, databases, machine learning, and more.

Google currently offers 11 certification exams for IT professionals to validate their GCP skills. Students can take the exam in person or online. Registration costs $99–200. The exams take between 90 min and 2 hours to complete and entail multiple-choice questions.

A wide range of learning resources, including courses and hands-on labs, are available on Coursera, Cloud Academy, A Cloud Guru, and Qwiklabs to prepare for the certification exams. Below, we’ve compiled more than 100 such resources that, we hope, will help you pass your Google certification.

How Google does Machine Learning
Google Cloud via Coursera
What is machine learning, and what kinds of problems can it solve? Google thinks about machine learning slightly differently — of being about logic, rather than just data. We talk about why such a framing is useful for data scientists when thinking about building a pipeline of machine learning models. Then, we discuss the five phases of converting a candidate use case to be driven by machine learning, and consider why it is important the phases not be skipped. We end with a recognition of the biases that machine learning can amplify and how to recognize this.

Starting from a history of machine learning, we discuss why neural networks today perform so well in a variety of data science problems.
