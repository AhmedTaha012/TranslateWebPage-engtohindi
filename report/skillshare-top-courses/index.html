[2023] 250 Top Skillshare Courses of All Time

We’ve compiled Skillshare’s 250 top online courses. Combined, they’ve accrued close to 7.9M enrollments.

Suparn Patra May 31st, 2022
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 0

Founded in 2010, Skillshare is one of the largest online learning platforms offering courses primarily on design, photography, writing, and other creative activities.

We’ve inspected Skillshare’s catalog, and here’s what we can tell you:

Skillshare boasts more than 41K online courses, with close to 35M enrollments and over 1.1M reviews combined. Over 1.3K of these courses have more than 50 lessons, with their longest course having 313 lessons. 

The platform has 13 million registered learners and over 500K paid subscribers. Their catalog subscription costs $168 per year or $32 per month. The platform’s top-500 teachers earn about $2,000 per month on average.

Since its inception, Skillshare has raised $136.8M; most notably, their raised $66M in 2020 in the wake of the pandemic’s onset, which remains their largest funding round to date.
